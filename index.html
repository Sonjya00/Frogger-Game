Welcome to my Frogger Game!

  • Bring your character to the other side of the board by clicking on the arrow keys.
  • Avoid the bugs running across the road. If they hit you, you will lose a life. When you lose all your lives, the game is over.
  • You can restore your lives by picking up the hearts on the board.
  • Collect as many gems as you can to get more points. Each gem type gives you a different amount of points.
  • Picking up stars can help you slow down the bugs, besides giving you extra points.
  • Every time you win, the bugs gets faster, and rocks will appear blocking your way from time to time, so beware!

To start, select a character, then press "Start a new game!"
