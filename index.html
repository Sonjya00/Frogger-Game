Welcome to my Frogger Game!

  • Bring your character to the other side of the board by clicking on the arrow keys. Beat all the 40 levels to win the game.
  • Avoid the bugs running across the road. If they hit you, you will lose a life. When you lose all your lives, the game is over.
  • Don't hesitate too much to make a move: if you run out of time, you'll lose the game.
  • Picking up items will boost your score and give you some help: hearts restore lives; stars slow down the bugs, and gems give you a different amount of points depending on their color.
  • Every time you win, the bugs gets faster, and rocks will appear blocking your way from time to time, so beware!

To start, select a character, then press "Start a new game!"
